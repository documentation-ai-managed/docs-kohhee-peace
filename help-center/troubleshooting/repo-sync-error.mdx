---
title: Repository Sync Error
description: Quickly resolve repository sync errors in Kohhee Peace.
---

## Issue
- Repository fails to sync
- Error: "Sync failed for repo"

## Causes
- Invalid credentials
- Insufficient permissions

## Solution
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check permissions">
    ````
    Ensure your access token has `repo` scope and admin access.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-authenticate">
    ````
    Logout and login again, or regenerate your token.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
